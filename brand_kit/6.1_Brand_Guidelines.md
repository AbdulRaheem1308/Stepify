# Wellnex Brand Guidelines

## Core Brand DNA
- **Tagline:** Move Better. Live Better.
- **Symbolism:** Stylized "W", Movement path, Growth progression, Wellness journey.
- **Visual Language:** Rounded corners, Soft gradients, White space, Premium enterprise feel, Modern health-tech aesthetic.

## Primary Colors
- **Navy Blue:** `#0B1D3A`
- **Teal:** `#0F766E`
- **Emerald Green:** `#10B981`
- **Accent Blue:** `#2563EB`
- **Light Gray:** `#F1F5F9`

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter (Sans-Serif)
- **Headings:** Inter Bold (800/700)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (400)
- **Enterprise Docs:** Inter Medium (500)

## Logo Rules
- **Minimum Size:** 24px height for digital, 0.5 inches for print.
- **Spacing (Clear Space):** Always maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "W" icon.
- **Incorrect Usage:**
  - Do not stretch or distort the logo.
  - Do not change the colors outside of the approved palette.
  - Do not place the dark logo on a dark background without sufficient contrast (use the Light Logo or Monochrome White instead).
  - Do not add drop shadows or effects to the flat logo assets.
