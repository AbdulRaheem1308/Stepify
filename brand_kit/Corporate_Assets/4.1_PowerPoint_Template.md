# Wellnex PowerPoint Template Structure

This document outlines the required structure and visual design for the official Wellnex corporate presentation template. Use the master assets provided in the `brand_kit` to construct these slides in PowerPoint or Keynote.

## Global Slide Master Settings
*   **Background:** `#F1F5F9` (Light Gray) for standard slides, `#0B1D3A` (Navy Blue) for transition/cover slides.
*   **Typography:** 
    *   Titles: `Inter Bold` (Color: `#0B1D3A` on light, `#FFFFFF` on dark)
    *   Body Text: `Inter Regular` (Color: `#334155` on light, `#FFFFFF` on dark)
*   **Footer:** Small horizontal logo (`1.1_Primary_Horizontal_Logo.svg`) in the bottom left, page number in the bottom right.

---

## Slide 1: Cover
*   **Background:** `#0B1D3A` (Dark Navy)
*   **Visuals:** Large centralized `1.5_Light_Logo.svg` (White text version).
*   **Text:** 
    *   Subtitle: "Move Better. Live Better."
    *   Bottom Right: Presenter Name & Date.

## Slide 2: Agenda
*   **Layout:** Two-column split.
*   **Left Column:** Large bold title "Agenda" with a vibrant `#10B981` (Emerald) underline.
*   **Right Column:** Numbered list (1-5) using `Inter Medium` font.

## Slide 3: Product
*   **Visuals:** Use the `2.5_Feature_Graphic.png` or `3.3_Hero_Illustration.png` on the right side.
*   **Text (Left):** "The Ultimate Wellness Companion." Highlight the walking dashboard and rewards system.

## Slide 4: Corporate Wellness
*   **Visuals:** Include the `1.3_Icon_Only_Logo.svg` in the top corner. 
*   **Text:** Focus on employee engagement, health analytics, and company-wide challenges. Use bullet points with custom green checkmarks.

## Slide 5: Insurance Integration
*   **Background:** `#0F766E` (Teal)
*   **Text:** "Lower Premiums Through Proven Activity." White typography explaining API hooks for health insurance providers.

## Slide 6: Pricing
*   **Layout:** Three-column comparison grid (Starter, Pro, Enterprise).
*   **Highlight:** Highlight the "Enterprise" tier using a `#2563EB` (Accent Blue) border and drop shadow.

## Slide 7: Thank You
*   **Background:** `#0B1D3A` (Dark Navy)
*   **Visuals:** `1.4_Dark_Logo.svg` centered.
*   **Text:** "joinwellnex.com" | Contact email | LinkedIn handle.
